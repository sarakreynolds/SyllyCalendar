SyllyCalendar is a student focused website designed to help students plan their scheudles.

Users can upload a pdf version of their course syllabi and our website will read your documents and then create a calendar with your courses:

* Assignments and their due dates

* Class Times

* Office Hours

Users can also add personal events as well as edit existing events.

This is currently a testing site and will be updated various times throughout the coming months
